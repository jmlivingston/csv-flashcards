CSV To Flashcards (Chrome / Firefox)

Instructions

  1. Click on "Upload From CSV" to load a CSV file or load some of the sample data. The CSV File must have columns in the following order with or without the header.
    • Order
    • Question
    • Answer
  2. Use your browser settings to print the first half of the cards.
  3. Feed the cards back into the printer and use your browser settings to print the second half.

Notes

  • The cards will popup in a separate tab. Your browser may prompt you to allow this.
  • Cards are printed so you can easily flip vertically instead of horizontally.
  • The order will be shown in the bottom right corner of each card.
  • The page number will be shown in the bottom right corner of each page.
